Project Overview

RoboRover is an advanced robotic rover designed for space exploration, remote terrain navigation, and hazardous environments. Powered by AI and equipped with cutting-edge sensors, it can perform missions autonomously, adapting to the conditions it encounters.

Rover Features

AI Navigation

Autonomous path planning, object detection, and obstacle avoidance for efficient exploration.

Multi-Sensor Vision

Equipped with cameras, LIDAR, and radar to create 3D maps of its surroundings in real-time.

Rugged Design

Designed to withstand extreme conditions, from deep space to harsh desert environments.

Applications

  • Space Exploration (Mars, Moon)
  • Disaster Relief Missions
  • Remote Terrain Mapping
  • Military Surveillance
